Disclosure Is Destined

At the Forefront of Disclosure

Here we make a brief introduction to some of the most credible eyewitnesses and inside sources. Ranging from alien abductees and Area 51 employees to former Pentagon officials and Navy Fighter Pilots, all sharing the same belief, UAPs are real.

Bob Lazar

Area 51 Whistleblower

In 1989 scientist Bob Lazar did an interview with news reporter George Napp revealing that he had worked on extra-terrestrial craft in Area 51. Many claims made by Lazar have since been verified as truthful.

Luis Elizondo

Pentagon Insider

Former director of the Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program, a program initiated by the Defence Intelligence Agency to study UAPs. Known for the release of the “Pentagon UFO Videos” in 2017. Elizondo has been instrumental in advancing disclosure and continues to provide fascinating insight into the subject of UAPs .

Commander Fravor

Tic-Tac Eyewitness

In 2004 Commander David Fravor received an order to intercept an unidentified aircraft. Piloting a super hornet fighter jet, Cmdr. Fravor located the UAP. He descried as appearing the same shape and colour of a tic-tac. The UAP began to circle away from Fravor’s jet before shooting off at an impossible speed.

Travis Walton

Alien Abductee

In November 1975, Travis Walton went missing and was assumed dead by authorities until that is, he showed up 5 days later, dehydrated, tired and confused but otherwise unharmed. After later undergoing hypnotic regression, Walton was able to recall being abducted by aliens after being incapacitated by some sort of energy emitted from a landed UAP.
